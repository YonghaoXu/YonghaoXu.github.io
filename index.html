Short Biography

I am currently a post-doctoral researcher at the Institute of Advanced Research in Artificial Intelligence (IARAI), working on AI4RS with Prof. Pedram Ghamisi. Before that, I received the Ph.D. degree in Photogrammetry and Remote Sensing from Wuhan University, advised by Prof. Liangpei Zhang and Prof. Bo Du. My research interests include remote sensing, computer vision, and machine learning.
